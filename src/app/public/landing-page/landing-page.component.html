About Us

Why Choose Kerr University?

Founded in 2022 by Digby R. Kerr, Kerr University offers personalized English as a Second Language (ESL) and business mentoring for learners at all levels. Our focus on conversational learning and practical teaching methods ensures you gain the skills and confidence needed to thrive in today's global environment.

Digby R. Kerr is a renowned business leader, mentor, and author with expertise in global logistics, advertising, recruitment, and business strategy. As a serial entrepreneur, he has built and led successful ventures, including Logistics Consulting, Inc. Beyond business, Digby is a passionate ESL tutor, empowering students worldwide to master English and excel professionally through his tailored mentorship, e-books, and courses.
